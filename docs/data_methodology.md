# Data Methodology — VibeSignal AI

## Why synthetic data

Real creator marketing data is proprietary and confidential.
Scraping from Instagram/YouTube/TikTok would violate Terms of Service.
VibeSignal AI uses synthetic data engineered to match published industry benchmarks.

## Engagement rate benchmarks used

| Tier  | Followers   | Our range  | Industry benchmark      |
|-------|-------------|------------|-------------------------|
| Nano  | 1K-10K      | 6-10%      | 6.23% avg (IMH 2026)    |
| Micro | 10K-100K    | 3-6%       | 3.86% avg               |
| Macro | 100K-1M     | 1-3%       | 1-2% range              |
| Mega  | 1M+         | 0.5-1.5%   | 1.21% avg               |

## What is NOT real in this dataset

Specific creator names, brand names, campaign names, revenue figures,
and individual conversion records are all algorithmically generated.
Row-level data is synthetic. Aggregate patterns are benchmark-grounded.
